About the rules

By using this website (the "site"), you agree that you'll follow these rules, and understand that if I think you haven't followed these rules, we will (at our discretion) take down these posts and terminate your access to the site.

Rules for @channel

  • Do not post anything generally NSFW. I would like to have a safe enough site that I wouldn't be disappointed to show it to friends or family.
  • Do not spam. You may post any content you want as long as it's legal. Just do not repost it multiple times in rapid succession.
  • Do not post personal information. This includes stuff like personal addresses, social security numbers, etc.
  • Do not post anything illegal. I feel like this should go without saying.
  • Do not post anything intentinally hateful. This includes discrimenatory comments, words, phrases, or anything mean in general targeted at specific people.
  • Do not break the site or do anything that interferes with normal usage of the site. Be sure to submit an issue on the Github repo if there are any bugs or security issues.
